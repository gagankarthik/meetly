Privacy by design

The only person watching your meetings is you.

No bots in your calls. No recordings to dial-in numbers. No third party seated at the table. Audio never leaves your machine — only the text you choose to keep is saved, encrypted, scoped to you.

  • Invisible to screen-share

    OS-level content protection — never gets captured by Zoom, Meet, Teams, or screen recorders.

  • Your data, your account

    Transcripts encrypted at rest, partitioned to your user. Even our infra can't see across accounts.

  • Delete anything, anytime

    Wipe a single meeting, all meetings, or your entire account from inside the app — no support ticket needed.

FAQ

Quick answers.

Will the other people on the call see Meetly?

No. The overlay uses native OS content-protection and stays invisible to Zoom, Google Meet, Teams, OBS, and screenshot tools.
Does Meetly join my calls as a bot?

Never. Meetly runs entirely on your computer and listens via your microphone and system audio. Other attendees are not notified — you're just using your laptop.
What languages does it support?

English first, with 30+ languages on the transcription engine. AI answers follow the language of the transcript.
Can I turn off recording?

Anytime. A single toggle in Settings makes every meeting ephemeral — nothing persists after you click Stop. Audio is never saved either way.
Which platforms?

Windows and macOS today. Linux is on the roadmap.
